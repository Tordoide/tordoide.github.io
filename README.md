# Tordoide Solutions

### IT consulting & software development

We help teams and founders turn strategy into working software — from advisory engagements through production-grade systems.

---

## What we do

**Consulting**  
Architecture reviews, technical due diligence, cloud and platform choices, delivery practices, and hands-on guidance when you need senior judgment without a full build.

**Software development**  
End-to-end delivery of non-trivial products: APIs, integrations, data pipelines, web and internal tools, and maintainable codebases built for change.

**Rapid MVP development**  
A focused path from idea to something real users can touch. We prioritize speed, clarity, and learning: lean scopes, working prototypes, and feedback loops so you validate assumptions before you over-invest. Ideal when you need to test a market, demo to stakeholders, or de-risk a concept with minimal waste.

---

## How we work

- **Aligned scope** — We agree on outcomes and constraints up front, then ship in small, reviewable steps.  
- **Transparent progress** — You always know what is done, what is next, and what we learned.  
- **Built to evolve** — Whether it is an MVP or a long-lived platform, we favor patterns that support iteration.
